My Background

Hello, my name is Annette Wiegold! I'm passionate about education, technology, and making a real difference in the lives of young learners. I've been working at the International School Ruhr in Essen since 2017- starting out as a Teaching Assistant in the Early Years, then moving into a German Teacher role, and now I'm a Homeroom Teacher. After volunteering with youth at my local church, I quickly realised how much I love guiding and inspiring young people. That feeling never left me, so I'm studying the ITEPS programme at NHL Stenden to become a fully qualified international primary school teacher- Early Years and Lower Primary are where my heart is.

My Hobbies

In my free time, I really enjoy growing my own vegetables in my small garden. Tending to my plants allows me to relax and connect with nature, and I find it very rewarding to watch my vegetables grow from seeds into fresh, healthy products. Gardening is not only a hobby but also a way for me to practice patience, creativity, and care, which are qualities I value both in my personal life and in my work with children.

I also enjoy going to metal concerts with friends. I love the energy and intensity of the music, as well as the excitement of being part of a live crowd. Experiencing different bands and their performances inspires me creatively and gives me a sense of adventure outside of my daily routine. Sharing these moments with friends makes it even more memorable and enjoyable.
